# Glossary

## BOE Area

Board of Education member area. Used for area-level summaries, maps, school lists, and Board reporting.

## Cluster

A school grouping or feeder-pattern context often used in district reporting.

## Promise Schools

Schools identified for additional support under Georgia Promise Schools-related reporting. In this project, Promise Schools are a key dashboard domain.

## Chronic Absenteeism

Typically the percentage of students missing 10% or more of enrolled school days. Exact definition should follow the district/state reporting standard used in the source data.

## Mobility Rate

A measure of student movement into or out of a school during the reporting period. Exact definition should be documented in the metric registry and methodology notes.

## Reading on Grade Level

An assessment-related reading indicator. Exact source and calculation should be documented in the metric registry.

## %P/D

Percent Proficient and Distinguished. Usually the percentage of tested students scoring Proficient Learner or Distinguished Learner.

## CCRPI

College and Career Ready Performance Index or CCRPI-style accountability data.

## ELSR / KREP

Early learning or kindergarten readiness-style reporting context used for early learning dashboards.

## Disclosure Review

A preview/review mode that flags small-n, suppression, privacy, and public-reporting interpretation risks.

## Accessibility Review

A preview/review mode that checks readability, color reliance, contrast, font size, and basic dashboard usability.
